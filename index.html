Welcome to RealDecoy’s Auction Site

RealAuction.ca is an online auction site provided to charities and community organizations looking for a fun and effective fund-raising tool. This tool is developed and supported by RealDecoy, a web applications and software development company with a long history of community involvement.

Please help support these organizations by visiting their auctions and bidding on the diverse items they have to offer. Remember that 100% of the funds raised through this site benefit those charities directly. Most of all – have fun!

To find out more about RealDecoy’s web development services or its commitment to community involvement, please visit www.RealDecoy.com.
